# Retirement & Investment Benefits
Retirement and investment savings programs are beneficial as they provide a structured way to save for the future, ensuring financial security during retirement. These programs often offer short and long-term tax advantages, and investment options, empowering you to build a nest egg and achieve long-term financial goals. {{ className: 'lead' }}

<Note>
 All programs are voluntary and you can change your contribution amount at any time.
</Note>

## 401(k) Program

Origin partners with Guideline to provide a 401(k) retirement savings plan that lets you invest a portion of each paycheck before taxes are deducted. Origin is covering 100% of the administration costs. Advisors are available to help discuss your options and retirement goals.
