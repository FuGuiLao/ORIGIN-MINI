## Investigation Division

The POST Regular Basic Course (RBC) is the entry-level training requirement for many California peace officers. The RBC Curriculum is divided into 42 individual topics, called Learning Domains. The Learning Domains contain the minimum required foundational information for given subjects, which are detailed in the Training and Testing Specification for Peace Officer Basic Courses. A separate workbook is available for each domain.
